MEET RAFA SEGNINI

Rafa began to feel the need of designing from the user's perspective, every time he used for the first time systems developed for industry. He realized that most colleagues shared the same frustrations and struggles as him.

After years of working in technical industrial fields, he discovered the UI / UX world, then a passion for designing highly usable products was awakened in him. He is convinced that UX / UI with solid statistics and data analysis produces precise measurable results that can be adjusted in the same way that an engineer calibrates a machine.

His ideal clients are those ones open to spot internal issues and explore new design solutions. All products can be analyzed and studied with the proper technique, the job of a UX designer is to find the correct procedure.
